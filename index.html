A Bit About Me

My name is Oscar, I am a game designer and artist hailing from Perth, Western Australia. I am currently double majoring in visual arts and game design at Edith Cowan Uni, while also helping run the tabletop games club there on the side. Across my breaks I get to spend most of my time prototyping board games, thinking up stories, and sketching stuff!

I hope to bring further attention and growth to the art and game design community in Western Australia throgh the creation of works with an emphasis on storytelling and strong themes created through these mediums.
